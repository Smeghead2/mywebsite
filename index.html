This site is designed to allow for easy learning of three mortal heros of Greek/Roman mythology. From the invincible Achilles to the death of Icarus, to the hero Perceus, this site features them. Each page will have an easy graph timeline, just for the very basic retelling of these time old stories.

This is by no means an official site to cite references from, but much of the stories here are accurate to the original myths.
